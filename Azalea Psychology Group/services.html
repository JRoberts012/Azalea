Emotion Dysregulation

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) is the gold-standard treatment for adolescents, adults, and children who experience intense and overwhelming emotions, relationship difficulties, and impulsivity. DBT combines mindfulness and acceptance-based approaches with principles of standard behavior therapy, with the ultimate goal of helping people learn to more skillfully manage emotions in order to ultimately build a life that is more satisfying and worth living. While we build a comprehensive DBT program, we currently provide DBT-informed individual and family therapy. Dr. Pincus is a certified DBT therapist by the DBT-Linehan Board of Certification.
