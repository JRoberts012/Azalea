Welcome To Our Practice:

Dr. Melanie Fox and Dr. Liza Pincus welcome you to Azalea Psychology Group, where compassionate, expert care is our top priority.

We believe in forming strong and genuine relationships with clients and families. Our ultimate goal is to help you create a life consistent with your values. We use evidence-based approaches to treat a variety of mental health concerns and address understandable reactions to life transitions and stressors. While we are a general private psychology practice, we are pleased to offer specialized practice areas including the treatment of eating disorders, emotion regulation difficulties, anxiety, obsessive-compulsive disorder, trauma, and parenting and relationship struggles. We also specialize in health and perinatal psychology. An integral part of our practice is collaborating with other providers in your life, including primary care doctors, schools, religious leaders, dieticians, etc. We believe in and are skilled at using evidence-based principles to tailor treatment to each individualâ€™s unique needs and backgrounds. We are proud to welcome clients of all ethnic, gender, sexual, and racial backgrounds to our practice.

The decision to create our group practice was simple: in addition to having worked on the same team at an academic medical center, we are childhood friends who genuinely love working together. Our practice is named after the beautiful azaleas that bloom each spring in the area where we grew up and supported one another through the ups and downs of adolescence.

We know that starting treatment can feel scary and that you may have had challenging therapy experiences in the past. We want you to know that we believe in bringing ourselves into the therapy room and breaking down traditional psychotherapy stereotypes. We strive to create a warm, welcoming environment, and we will encourage your feedback. We take an active, behavioral approach to treatment and we are results-driven. Our goal is for you to see measurable progress, not to remain in treatment forever!
